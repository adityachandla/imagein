My name is prakriti khurana and i study in BFIA in Shaheed Sukhdev college of business studies. I love to paint in my spare time, which is very less. This website is a collection of some of my paintings, I would love to hear what you think about them. Press Like if you like them and leave a comment if you have something to say about it. Also feel free to contact me via the message box below.
I have been painting since as long as I can remember and during that time I have tried to experiment with as many drawing forms as I could. I have made paintings using watercolors, graphite and charcoal. I also like to experiment with paintings on body as some of the images here might indicate.
Except painting I am also involved in social work as part of an organization named Enactus. As part of that I help with projects that focus on betterment of children in backward parts of the city. I have also been a part of the music club of my college and won various national level competetions in acapella. I am also an avid reader and debator.
